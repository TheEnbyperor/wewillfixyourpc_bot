Types of Data collected

The Users's first and last name, and profile picture will be collect for the purposes being shown to live agents to provide the requested services, if and when the Users initiates a conversation with the Business.

In the case of placing an order the Users phone number and email address will be stored for the purposes of facilitating further contact to update the User on their order progress.

In the case of taking card payments the users card number, expiry date, and CVC will be collected by the Application but will not be stored for any time longer than absolutely required to process said payment. This data may be sent to third party payment processors to complete the payment and will be processed according to their privacy policy. The User may request which payment processors their data has been sent to at any time by contacting the Data Controller above.
The Application will not store the card details on file itself or with any third party unless explicitly stated by the application at the time of collecting card details and requested by the User, excluding the last 4 digits of the card number to allow the User to identify what payment method they used.

If the Application requests the User's IMEI it will only be used to facilitate the requested actions and will not be used for further actions not requested by the User at the time of providing their IMEI.
